Product overview
Overview
Icon 100CS is an insecticide with advanced capsule suspension formulation technology to control
malaria vector in humans (Anopheles aconitus), flies and cockroaches (Blatella germanica).
Key Features
• Broad spectrum insecticide
• iCAP technology formulation, ensures protection of the active ingredient
• Effective with low rate application dose
Key Benefit
• Providing a long-lasting residual control
• Recommended for indoor spraying by WHOPES (WHO Pesticides Evaluation Scheme)
• Excellent acceptance with low odor and no-staining
• Economical, very effective with low rate application dose
